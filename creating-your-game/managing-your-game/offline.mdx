### How can I create on Soba offline?

Yes, it's possible to keep creating games on Soba without internet!

1. Open the Soba app once while being connected to the internet.
2. Lose access to the internet, but keep the app open.
3. Navigate to your creations.
4. Select any game that you were previously creating (that game is saved locally on your phone storage).
5. Continue creating your game, even with no internet access!
6. When re-establishing internet connection, open the game you were creating and push its new changes through the “Posting Settings” button and then “Post game".
