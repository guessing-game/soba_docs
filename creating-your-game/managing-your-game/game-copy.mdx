### Here’s how to make a copy of your game:

1. Navigate to the “My Creations” screen.
    
<p align="center">
  <img width="700" noZoom src="https://i.imgur.com/sw3By0C.png" />
</p>
    
2. To make a copy of a [game](https://wiki.soba.xyz/creating-your-game/managing-your-game/save-game), tap on the three dots located next to the game's name.
3. Tap on “Make a copy". 
4. The game will be duplicated to your creations.
5. Rename the game and use it as you wish!
