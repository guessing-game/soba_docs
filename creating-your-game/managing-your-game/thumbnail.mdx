### How do I set a thumbnail?

1. In the World editor, tap the orange icon on the top left corner of the screen
    
<p align="center">
  <img width="130" noZoom src="https://i.imgur.com/TocBoa7.png" />
</p>
    
2. Tap on the ‘Posting Settings’ option.
3. Tap on the small “Set thumbnail” text located on top of the default thumbnail image.
4. Move your camera using one or two fingers. You can also zoom in and zoom out to find the angle you wish for the thumbnail image.
    
<p align="center">
  <img width="600" noZoom src="https://i.imgur.com/79F8GOX.png" />
</p>
    
5. Tap on the button icon “Set thumbnail". 
6. Post your game for the changes to reflect your game in the live version.
