### How to delete my game?

1. Navigate to the “My Creations” screen.
    
<p align="center">
  <img width="500" noZoom src="https://i.imgur.com/B254pP3.png" />
</p>
    
2. Tap on the three dots by the side of the name of the game that you want to make a copy.
3. Tap on “Delete”. 
4. ❗The game will be deleted from your creations, with no way to restore it.
