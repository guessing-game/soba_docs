### How do I change my game name?

1. In the World editor, tap the orange icon on the top left corner of the screen

<p align="center">
  <img width="130" noZoom src="https://i.imgur.com/TocBoa7.png" />
</p>
    
2. Tap on the ‘Posting Settings’ option.
3. Tap on your game name, which is right above the “Post Game” button.
    
<p align="center">
  <img width="300" noZoom src="https://i.imgur.com/OBxmlsZ.png" />
</p>
    
4. Type the new name.
5. Post the game for the changes to reflect your game in the live version
