- **Pan:** Move one finger across the screen to pan to move the camera parallel to the ground
- **Tilt:** Change camera angle using two fingers
- **Zoom:** Pinch the screen using your two thumbs to zoom
- **Rotate:** Make a circling movement with your two thumbs to rotate the screen
