---
title: 'Are all games multiplayer?'
sidebarTitle: 'Are all games multiplayer?'
description: ''
---

No! On Soba you can create both multiplayer and single-player games, as well as set the maximum amount of players that can play your game at the same time. To configure such options, refer to the [How to adjust the game rule settings?](creating-your-game/managing-your-game/rules) guide.
