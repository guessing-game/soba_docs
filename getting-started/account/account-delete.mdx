---
title: 'How do I delete my account?'
description: ''
---

If you decide to delete your account, please follow these steps:

1. Login into your [Soba](https://wiki.soba.xyz/getting-started/getting-started/multi-platform) account
2. Go to the Setting from your profile section, you need to tap this ⚙️ button
3. Tap “Delete Account” button and confirm deletion on the pop-up screen

Account deletion is a permanent action. Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

#### What happens with my games? 

- All your published games will be kept on the platform but they will be anonymized; meaning they will be published undo anonymous user.
- All your unpublished games will be deleted along with all your personal information.

If you have just played an never created an account - then there is nothing to delete. You can just uninstall the app if you don’t want to use Soba any longer.
