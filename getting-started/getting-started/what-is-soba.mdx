Soba is an online multiplayer gaming platform with integrated no-code game making tools. Everyone can easily create their own games on mobile, **without needing to know how to code**, and play games that others have created. 

The app works on mobile (iOS, Android) and computer (Windows and Mac) so you can create and play games from any of your devices!
