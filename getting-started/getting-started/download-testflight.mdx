Currently, our app isn't available on the App Store in regions outside **the UK, Germany, and Canada**. But don't worry, you can still get early access to our app through a few simple steps from your phone:

1. **Install TestFlight:** Begin by downloading [TestFlight](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/testflight/id899247664) from Apple. This is Apple's official app for beta testing. Our app, Soba, is available for beta testing through TestFlight.


2. **Join the Soba Beta:** After installing TestFlight, use this [link](https://testflight.apple.com/join/PJAJGNJP) to become a beta tester for Soba. Welcome aboard!
