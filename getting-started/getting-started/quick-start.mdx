There are many ways to start at Soba, but if you cannot wait to start your creation journey, here’s how to start:

1. Open your creation section by tapping “Create” button on the Discovery screen
2. Choose a [template](https://wiki.soba.xyz/creating-your-game/general/what-template) and load up your game; you are now in the World Building space!
3. Familiarise yourself with [camera controls](https://wiki.soba.xyz/creating-your-game/general/controls)
4. Customise your game by [adding objects](https://wiki.soba.xyz/creating-your-game/world-building/place-objects) to your game from the sidebar
5. Add your first [behavior](https://wiki.soba.xyz/behavior-editor/general/blocks)
6. Playtest your Game to see whether everything works as you planned
7. Publish your Game!
