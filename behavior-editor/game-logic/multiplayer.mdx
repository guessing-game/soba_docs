### How to set my game to multiplayer or single-player?

1. Tap on the orange icon on the top left corner of the screen.
<p align="center">
  <img width="130" noZoom src="https://i.imgur.com/TocBoa7.png" />
</p>
2. Tap on “Multiplayer Settings”.
2. Enable "Multiplayer” to make your game multiplayer, or disable to make it single-player.
