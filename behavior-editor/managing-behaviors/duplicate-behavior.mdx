---
title: 'How do I duplicate a behavior?'
description: 'Save time with duplicating behaviors to your objects'
---
To copy/paste a [behavior](https://wiki.soba.xyz/behavior-editor/managing-behaviors/behavior-object), follow these simple steps:

1. Tap on the object which has the behavior you wish to copy
2. Open the side-panel with details by tapping “Details” in the left-hand side of screen.
3. To copy the behavior, drag the card to the left. This will copy the behavior to your clipboard.
4. Choose a new object where you want to paste this behavior. 
5. On the sidebar of this new object, tap “Paste Behavior” on its sidebar. Your behavior is pasted!
