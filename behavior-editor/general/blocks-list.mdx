<Accordion title="What are Blocks" icon="rectangle-history">
    [Blocks](https://wiki.soba.xyz/behavior-editor/general/blocks) are the puzzle pieces on our behavior editor. These blocks connect to form a column, with each piece representing an action for the mechanic you're creating. See image below for understanding what are blocks.
